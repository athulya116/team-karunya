About Us

Team Karunya is a trusted provider of comprehensive services, including ambulance services, mobile mortuary services, and hire services to the communities of Parassala and Kaliakkavilai. Our team is dedicated to ensuring that people receive the critical support they need during emergencies, life events, and important occasions. From providing prompt medical assistance to offering professional event equipment rentals, Team Karunya is here to serve with reliability, professionalism, and care.

Our ambulance services are available 24/7, ensuring that emergency medical assistance reaches patients swiftly. With a fleet of well-equipped ambulances featuring basic life support systems, oxygen facilities, and first aid kits, we provide immediate care during critical situations such as accidents, medical emergencies, and labor.

In addition to ambulance services, Team Karunya offers mobile mortuary services for dignified transportation and preservation of deceased individuals. Our mobile mortuaries are equipped with the necessary facilities to ensure that loved ones are handled with utmost respect and care.

Beyond medical and emergency services, Team Karunya also provides hire services for various occasions such as weddings, funerals, and other functions. From chairs and tables to shamiyanas, tents, and lighting, we ensure that every event is well-organized and beautifully arranged. Our team works closely with clients to meet their specific needs, offering flexible rental options, timely setup, and exceptional service to make every event memorable and stress-free.
